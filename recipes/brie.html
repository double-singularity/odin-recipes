Baked Stuffed Brie with Cranberries & Walnuts

Ingredient

  • 1 small wheel of brie (about 6 to 8 inches), chilled
  • ¼ cup dried cranberries
  • ¼ cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed, plus extra for (optional) design
  • 1 egg, beaten with
  • 1 teaspoon water

Step by step instruction

  1. Score the side of a wheel of brie all the way around with a sharp paring knife.
  2. Press the dried cranberries on one cut side of the brie, and the walnuts on the other.
  3. Roll out a thawed sheet of puff pastry on a floured surface to about 1/8-inch thickness. Place brie in center of pastry.
  4. Lightly brush the decorative pieces with egg wash.
  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Place the brie on the prepared baking sheet. Bake on the center rack in preheated oven until it is browned and leaking cheese.
